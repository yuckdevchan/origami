Paper.io Private Server

A new era is coming to the universe of Paper.io 2. Play with your friends instead of random strangers by sharing a simple link. For years gamers have enjoyed capturing territories by coloring empty and occupied areas. Now it’s time to experience this legendary gameplay in a new way. Invite people you know to your private arena, and have fun without trolls and griefers. Find out who is the most gifted conqueror in the group.

In gaming, certain mechanics never get old. This IO game is just as entertaining and enjoyable as ever. Players mark their borders by moving around a giant blank sheet. Every time the outline comes full circle, the area within it is captured. The goal is to paint as much of the map into your color as possible. The leader board is updated in real-time and displayed at the top right corner. Protect your domain, take lands away from rivals, and fight for the first place.
