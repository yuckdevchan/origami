Challenges Paper.io

Paper.io has been around for a while and millions of players around the world have been enjoying this game for what feels like forever. It simply doesn't get old! But what if you could re-experience the joy of discovering Paper for the first time? Or what if you simply want more of this awesomeness in your life? Well, you are in luck, because we are proud to introduce a new game mode called Challenges!

So you might be wondering, what exactly these challenges are? To put it simply, they are exactly what they sound like: there are multiple levels of varying difficulty, each containing a special task the player needs to achieve. The basic gameplay principles remain the same, but now the player will be required to capture a certain percentage of the map or defeat a certain number of opponents to complete the challenge.

But that's not all! We've introduced new fun mechanics to spice things up even more. Some of the levels will have certain gimmicks to them, like inverted controls, high speed mode or slow mode.

Do you think you are good enough at Paper.io to complete all of the challenges? Time to experience everything this game has to offer and prove that you have what it takes!
